About Us

IEEE

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

IEEE and its members inspire a global community to innovate for a better tomorrow through highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities. IEEE is the trusted "voice" for engineering, computing, and technology information around the globe.

-IEEE Mission Statements

UAlbany IEEE Student Branch

The University at Albany IEEE Student branch is the hub for all students who ask for more from the computers in their lives.

Our mission is to advocate for the advancement of technology and share with others the value of mastering its many arts. We host technical workshops, coding interviews, hackathons, and special guests, all while maintaining a tight knit and welcoming community of professionals.

We provide the means necessary to bridge the gap, connecting with both local and big tech alike, such as Google, Cisco, and Global Foundries, and provide students access to presentations on cutting edge research from our university's very own faculty.
